Pristine Beauty Unleashed

From the depths of Lake Tanganyika to the peaks of Mount Heha, Burundi offers landscapes that are as diverse as they are breathtaking. Immerse yourself in the green heart of the continent.

Lake Tanganyika

Burundi borders the world's longest freshwater lake. A biological treasure trove, it is home to hundreds of endemic species and legends like Gustave the crocodile. Its beaches are the perfect escape.

Emerald National Parks

Explore the lush Kibira Forest or the expansive plains of Ruvubu. Burundi's national parks are sanctuaries for wildlife, from wild chimpanzees to majestic hippos roaming the riverbanks.

The Source of the Nile

In the heart of Burundi lies the southernmost source of the world's longest river. The pyramid at Rutovu marks the beginning of a journey that flows through the history of civilizations.
